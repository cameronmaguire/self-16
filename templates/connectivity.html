Connectivity

Connecting and hosting the SELF

The SELF® is not able to be connected, either electronically or mechanically, into aircraft systems – it is a passive passenger on the aircraft – very low or no certification requirements.
It does not affect or duplicate any of the future intended connectivity concepts.

Ability to be Retro Fitted

The SELF® is able to be retro-fit installed on any existing aircraft almost immediately – precise locations need to be agreed.

Requirement of Self Application

An on-going cost of satellite-based email facility is also required – but this does depend on the number of devices and messages.
Battery life is +2years due to the large dormant phase of operation, so maintenance can be included in standard 2-year industry cycles.
